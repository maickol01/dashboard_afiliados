# Initial Concept
Electoral management dashboard for hierarchical campaign management and analytics.

# Product Guide

## 1. Target Audience
*   **Campaign Managers & Political Leaders:** To oversee strategy, high-level performance, and resource allocation.
*   **Data Analysts & Electoral Strategists:** To dig into demographics, trends, and optimize targeting.
*   **Volunteers & Field Workers:** (Implied) To capture data and be tracked for productivity.

## 2. Core Features & Goals
*   **Consolidated Analytics Dashboard:**
    *   **Citizen Registration:** Real-time counters of total registered citizens.
    *   **Performance Tracking:** Detailed metrics for Leaders, Brigadiers, and Mobilizers (productivity, goals met).
*   **Geographic Analysis (Navojoa, Sonora):**
    *   **Interactive Map:** A visual representation of Navojoa.
    *   **Electoral Sections:** The map must be segmented by official electoral sections.
    *   **Citizen Visualization:** Layer showing the location/density of registered citizens on the map.
*   **Data Quality Assurance:**
    *   **Validation Metrics:** Dashboards/reports to ensure all citizen data is being entered correctly and fully.
*   **Hierarchical Management:**
    *   **CRUD Operations:** A hierarchical table interface to administer (Create, Read, Update, Delete) database records directly in Supabase.

## 3. Success Metrics
*   Accurate and real-time visualization of campaign progress.
*   Successful integration of the Navojoa electoral map.
*   High data integrity through quality monitoring.
*   Efficient team management via performance tracking.
